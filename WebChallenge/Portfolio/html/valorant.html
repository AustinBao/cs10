VALORANT "Aimbot"

Introduction

VALORANT is a tactical FPS videogame with "precise" gunplay. In this project, I aimed to train a YOLOv8 model that predicts and tracks enemy Agents in your field of view. A possible continuation for this project is to connect to an Arduino or Raspberry Pi and allow the model to aim for you (Disclaimer: do not use in ranked; you will get permanently banned).
