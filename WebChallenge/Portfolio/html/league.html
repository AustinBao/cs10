League of Legends Jungler Tracker

Introduction

With my current experiences playing the videogame, League of Legends, a multiplayer MOBA developed by Riot Games, I realized the importance of a good Jungler. Their influence can completely change the outcome of games. An important factor that all great Junglers consider is the enemy Jungler's position, Therefore, as a Jungler myself, I created a YOLOv8 model that detects and tracks the enemy Jungler's position on the map.
